A Breath of Fresh Air: Champ de Mars

Champ de Mars, a sprawling green expanse in the heart of Paris, offers a welcome respite from the city's bustling energy. This historic park has served as a recreational ground, military parade field, and a gathering place for Parisians for centuries.

Unwinding and Exploring

Champ de Mars offers a variety of activities for visitors to enjoy:

  • Picnic on the sprawling lawns: Spread out a blanket, savor a delicious picnic lunch, and soak in the Parisian atmosphere.
  • Stroll or jog along the manicured paths: Enjoy a leisurely walk or jog amidst the fresh air and scenic views.
  • Marvel at the Eiffel Tower: Champ de Mars offers some of the best perspectives of the iconic Eiffel Tower, perfect for capturing stunning photographs.
  • Visit the Wall for Peace: This art installation commemorates peace and serves as a thought-provoking landmark within the park.
  • People-watch and soak in the atmosphere: Observe locals enjoying their leisure time, from families playing frisbee to children riding bicycles.

Getting There and Exploring Further

Champ de Mars is conveniently located in the 7th arrondissement of Paris and easily accessible by public transportation:

  • Metro lines 8 (Ecole Militaire station) and C (Champ de Mars – Tour Eiffel station) stop near the park.
  • Several bus lines also serve the area, offering a flexible travel option.
  • Consider renting bicycles from a nearby Velib' station for an active exploration of the park and surrounding areas.

After enjoying Champ de Mars, you can explore nearby attractions like the Eiffel Tower, the Palais Bourbon (French National Assembly), and the Musée Rodin.
